Overview

Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY) is the world's largest government-funded health insurance scheme. It aims to provide health insurance coverage to poor and vulnerable households, promoting healthy lives and well-being for all.

  • Urological procedures
  • Gastroenterology procedures
  • Oncology (Cancer treatment)
  • Other secondary and tertiary surgical procedures

Eligibility Criteria

Automatic Eligibility:

  • Below Poverty Line (BPL) families as per SECC 2011
  • Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe households
  • Selected families from landless labor households

How to Use the Scheme

Step Process
1 Visit empaneled hospital with identity proof
2 Hospital verifies your eligibility in PMJAY portal
3 You receive treatment at zero cost
4 Hospital gets reimbursed directly by government

Empaneled Hospitals

The scheme covers treatment at over 24,000+ empaneled hospitals across India including public and private facilities.

Impact & Statistics

  • Coverage: Over 50 crore (500 million) beneficiaries
  • Saved thousands of crores in out-of-pocket medical expenses
  • Free treatment for critical surgeries and procedures
  • Reduced financial burden on poor families
