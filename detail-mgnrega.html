Overview

MGNREGA (Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act) is a social security and public works scheme in India. It aims to provide livelihood security to households in rural areas by guaranteeing 100 days of wage employment in a financial year. The scheme also promotes social inclusion through building infrastructure and natural resource management.

Key Guarantee: 100 days of paid employment per household per financial year

Key Features

  • 100 Days Guarantee: Minimum 100 days of wage employment per financial year
  • Work Site Facilities: Drinking water, rest shed, and basic medical facilities
  • Skills Development: Training provided to workers in various fields
  • Social Audit: Transparent monitoring and public accountability
  • Infrastructure Development: Creating lasting community assets
  • Unemployment Allowance: Unemployment benefit if work not provided

Eligibility & Registration

Who can apply:

  • All rural households in India
  • Both men and women can register
  • No age limit (except below minimum wage age)
  • Registration is free
  • Self-identification (no official documentation required for registration)

How to Get Work

Step Process
1 Register at Gram Panchayat (village council)
2 Get Job Card issued (free)
3 Apply for work at Gram Panchayat
4 Work is provided within 15 days
5 Wages paid through bank transfer

Benefits to Beneficiaries

  • Guaranteed employment during lean season
  • Stable income for poor households
  • Skills development and training
  • Community asset creation
  • Social inclusion and dignity of work
  • Unemployment benefits if work not provided
  • Equal wages for men and women

Employment Statistics

  • Over 13 crore (130 million) households employed annually
  • Created over 7000+ assets in communities
  • Generated livelihoods during rural agricultural off-season
  • Provided skills to millions of workers
