Overview

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PM Awas Yojana) is a housing scheme launched to provide affordable housing for all citizens of India. The scheme aims to address the housing shortage in both urban and rural areas by providing credit-linked subsidy, direct assistance, and beneficiary-led construction.

Objective: To provide a pucca (permanent) house with basic amenities to all households by 2024

Key Features

  • Credit-Linked Subsidy: Direct subsidy on home loans up to ‚Çπ2.67 lakh
  • Low Interest Rates: Reduced rate of interest on home loans
  • Easy Loan Processing: Faster approval and disbursement
  • Minimal Documentation: Simplified application process
  • Urban & Rural Coverage: Available in both cities and villages
  • Flexible Repayment: Extended repayment period

Scheme Components

Component Description
Credit-Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS) Subsidy on home loans for LIG/EWS families
Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP) Public-private partnership for housing development
Beneficiary-Led Construction (BLC) Individual construction with government assistance
In-Situ Slum Rehabilitation (ISSR) Rehabilitation of slum dwellers with free housing

Direct Benefit Transfer:

  • EWS/LIG: Up to ‚Çπ2.67 lakh subsidy on home loans

Application Process

Step Process
1 Register on official portal at pmaymis.gov.in
2 Fill application form with necessary details
3 Submit required documents (ID, Income proof, etc.)
4 Application undergoes verification
5 Approval and bank loan processing
6 Subsidy disbursed directly to bank

Housing Specifications

  • Pucca house with basic amenities
  • Built with earthquake-resistant design
  • Access to clean water and sanitation
  • Electricity connection
  • Size varies based on approved norms
  • Environment-friendly construction

Success & Impact

  • Over 2 crore (20 million) houses sanctioned
  • Over 1 crore (10 million) houses completed
  • Transformed housing sector in India
  • Generated employment in construction
  • Improved living conditions of poor families

Rural vs Urban Components

Urban PM Awas Yojana: Focuses on credit-linked subsidy and slum rehabilitation

Rural PM Awas Yojana: Provides direct financial assistance for house construction
